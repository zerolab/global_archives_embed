Global Archives Embed

The Global Archives Embed widget is a simple, quick embed for Threatened Voices, a collaborative mapping project to build a database of bloggers who have been threatened, arrested or killed for speaking out online and to draw attention to the campaigns to free them.

The widget currently uses a bundled Simile Widgets Timeline v2.4-dev, TimeMap.js v1.6 and a stripped down, cleaned version of the initialization scripts from Threatened Voices.

Embed

To use the widget, simply copy the code below and paste it on your site. The widget requires a minimum of 400px width and height, because of its map + timeline mashup nature
